# Product Vision
Help students achieve the dream of topping the charts of JEE mains. 
The service should list the subjects, lessons, topics. What are the cross-refernces of each topic. (The cross references could be outside the subjects as well). All the topics should have examples as well. All the topics and subtopics has to be graded (hard, medium, easy).
For each topic there will be an assessment (or quiz or a problem to solve). The user will submit the answer/solution (or the picture of handwrittensolution). The system will evaluate the answer and give where exactly the user stands in his preparation and what should be the exact remedy/fixes to the answer to go to next level. 
The level is determined by every answer he submits. The user will be graded for each topic. Even if the user has answered correctly, the system has to provide easier ways (shortcuts to arrive at solution) categorised by time, complexity.

## Problem Statement
User need to understand the cross-references of the topics, which helps the user to understand and score better in the exams.
User may not afford the personal coaching, where he understand whether his answer is the right method of solving or not.

## Target Audience
students in India who are preparing for JEE mains
Students who are just passed 10 (SSLC) and are looking for guidance on how to prepare for JEE mains. This is specific to STEM.

## Market Opportunity
Target for the low-hanging fruit: with small investment (with as little as 999 they can access), we can reach out to a large number of students who are preparing for JEE mains. 

## Why Now
The competition is huge, but there is a gap in the market for a personalized report based on the current preparation level of the students. With the advancements in AI and machine learning, it is now possible to provide personalized feedback and recommendations to students based on their performance.
Each topic and subtopic cross-refereences are not available in the books, which is essential for the students to understand the concepts and score better in the exams. 

## Competitive Landscape
There is huge competition (at least there are 100 established players in the market). All of them are providing similar services except that they are not providing a personalized report based on the current preparation level of the students. They are not providing the cross-references of the topics and subtopics, which is essential for the students to understand the concepts and score better in the exams.
