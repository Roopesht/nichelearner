## Summary

A dedicated page that explains how colleges can deploy NicheLearner for their students. It communicates the institutional value proposition (bulk access, trial terms, measurable outcomes, integration ease), addresses college administrator concerns, and clearly explains the partnership pathway.

## User Story

As a college administrator, I want to understand how NicheLearner works at scale and what the partnership process looks like, so I can evaluate whether to offer it to my students.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **Page is discoverable from homepage** — college administrators can find this page via "For Institutions," "College Partnerships," or similar link from the homepage
- **Institutional value proposition is clear** — page explains how NicheLearner fills gaps in the college's test-prep offering and why cross-topic mapping and personalized assessment matter
- **College concerns are addressed** — page explains: how bulk deployments work, what data colleges can access, how trials are managed at scale, what support is provided
- **Trial terms for institutions are documented** — page clarifies how many students can trial, trial duration, and what happens after trial ends
- **Contact/inquiry pathway is clear** — page provides obvious way for college admins to express interest (link to inquiry form or email)
- **Differentiation from competitors is explained** — page explains how NicheLearner differs from other JEE prep platforms (based on product differentiation documented elsewhere)
- **No detailed course content is listed** — page does not enumerate subjects or topics (that's in the product)
- **Page includes college-specific messaging** — addresses concerns like institutional scale, bulk pricing, student outcome measurement, integration requirements

## Priority

**High** — College partnerships are the primary near-term acquisition channel; without a credible institutional page, college outreach will fail.

## Dependencies

- `homepage` (college partnerships page is linked from homepage)
- `college-partnership-inquiry-form` (page must link to inquiry form)
