# Solution: NicheLearner

## Product Vision

Students preparing for JEE Mains in India lack personalized guidance that connects topics across subjects. Current market offerings provide generic content without cross-referencing and without realistic feedback on individual preparation levels. Many students cannot afford personal coaching to understand whether they are solving problems using the right methods. This creates a gap: students have no way to assess their true readiness and get concrete guidance on what to learn next.

## Why Now

Recent advancements in AI and machine learning now make personalized feedback and adaptive learning paths feasible at scale. While 100+ competitors exist in the JEE prep market, none provide two critical gaps the market demands: (1) cross-topic reference mapping that shows how concepts connect across subjects, and (2) personalized preparation reports matched to the student's actual proficiency level rather than generic self-help.

## Core Value Proposition

**Personalized proficiency-matched assessment** — quizzes automatically adapt to each student's level, not one-size-fits-all.

**Cross-topic reference navigation** — every topic maps to related concepts across subjects, filling a gap that textbooks leave empty.

**Adaptive next-step recommendations** — each student receives concrete, ranked remediation paths based on where they actually stand, not where they think they stand.
