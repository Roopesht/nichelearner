# Solution: User Personas

## Persona: JEE Aspirant (Student)

**Who they are:** A high school student in India, typically post-10th grade, preparing for the JEE Mains entrance examination to secure admission to an engineering college.

**What they want:** A realistic picture of their current preparation level relative to the exam. Clear identification of which topics matter most and how concepts interconnect across subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics). A personalized roadmap of what to study next, prioritized by their specific gaps and confidence level.

**What they fear:** Wasting months studying inefficiently or focusing on the wrong topics. Realizing after the exam that they used suboptimal solving methods when shortcuts existed. Unable to afford private coaching to validate whether their approach is correct. Arriving at exam day unprepared because they had no honest feedback on their readiness.

**How they relate:** Depends on parents for financial and emotional support; may be part of an institutional trial program through their college; studies independently but seeks validation of their learning strategy.

---

## Persona: Parent

**Who they are:** A parent of a high school student preparing for JEE Mains, typically in India, concerned with their child's future career prospects and educational outcomes.

**What they want:** Confidence that their child is on track to succeed in the exam. Transparency into their child's actual preparation level and progress. Assurance they are investing wisely in their child's education—whether through tutoring, courses, or platforms. Peace of mind that their child is not falling behind or wasting time on ineffective study methods.

**What they fear:** Their child failing the JEE Mains exam and missing the opportunity to attend a top engineering college. Spending money on coaching or resources that don't improve outcomes. Discovering too late that their child was unprepared or struggled with fundamental concepts. Feeling helpless because they cannot directly assess whether their child is learning effectively.

**How they relate:** Makes or influences the decision to try the product (especially free trials); may monitor their child's progress and engagement; seeks assurance that their investment in education is paying off.
